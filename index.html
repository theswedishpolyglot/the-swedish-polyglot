About

William Matkaselkä started learning languages in 2016 and has been hooked ever since. Today his repertoire of languages span three language groups and include Swedish, English, German, Spanish, Portuguese, French, Italian, Japanese, Mandarin and Vietnamese.

In his pursuit of higher levels of comprehension in these languages, he has developed an interest in psychology and technology as means to becoming more effective.

Since 2023 he holds a master's degree in psychology from Linköping University and he is currently pursuing a degree in software engineering in his free time.

He has been teaching language learning methodology and specific languages such as Swedish, English and Spanish since 2018. He has now taught over 2 800 lessons to more than 600 students in over 50 countries.

He is currently involved in several projects meant to advance the activities of language learning and second-language acquisition:

  • He is writing a book with the working title Language Learning From First Principles. It currently spans over 100 000 words and 400 pages. He has written an average of 500 words or 2 paragraphs per day.
  • He is developing language learning software that automates the creation of large, effective and compelling flashcard decks. He is currently selling the products of this software through his teacher profile as well as on his website. The software will be available as one or more services or applications in the future. He has written 10 000 lines of code in the first 6 months of development.

Services

William is currently offering his language services over on his teacher profile. These services include:

  • Language Lessons: Learn the contents of specific languages, such as Swedish, English and Spanish. After each lesson you recieve a flashcard deck with 10 card types and an audio file containing all the sentences from the lesson. The flashcard deck includes sentence translations, individual word definitions, sentence audio etc.
  • Metalearning Lessons: Learn how to learn and improve your grasp of effective language learning methodology.
  • Custom Flashcards: Order a frequency-based flashcard deck containing some arbitrary number of high-frequency words in your target language complete with context sentences, sentence translations, individual word definitions and sentence audio. Alternatively you can order a flashcard deck containing all the unique words, phrases and sentences from any given book (or set of books), movie (or set of movies), episode (or season) or set of YouTube-videos.

Shop

William is currently offering his language learning products over on his teacher profile and on Gumroad. These products include:

  • Frequency-Based Flashcard Decks: Includes some number of high-frequency phrases in the target language, from 1 000 to 30 000 depending on your preference and the language in question. These decks typically have a card type for reading, a card type for active listening and a card type for speaking (cloze cards). Each card has the target phrase in the context of a sentence, a sentence translation, sentence audio and individual word definitions. One example of a frequency-based deck on offer is the Swedish 2k deck:
    • Click to see Swedish 2k deck
  • Content-Based Flashcard Decks: Includes all unique phrases and sentences from a piece or set of content of your choosing. This can be a book, a movie, a season of a TV-series, a set of YouTube videos etc. The deck will have card types for reading, active listening and speaking (cloze cards). Each card has a context sentence, sentence translation, sentence audio and individual word definitions.
  • Large Dictionary Spreadsheets: A dictionary sheet containing detailed information on some number of lemma and non-lemma in the target language (e.g. 60 000 to 200 000 unique words depending on the language). Information on each word includes pronunciation data, definitions, grammatical information etc.
