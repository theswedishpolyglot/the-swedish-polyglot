Why should I book lessons with The Swedish Polyglot?

  • Your teacher has language learning expertise: your teacher has himself, since 2016, learned multiple languages to an intermediate level or higher (including for example Spanish, German and Japanese) and spends over 2 000 hours per year studying languages himself (reading, listening etc.).
  • Your teacher has given over 3 300 lessons to more than 700 students: with an average rating of 5 out of 5.
  • After each lesson you receive a flashcard deck and and audio file containing all sentences: to reinforce the new reading, listening and speaking vocabulary (as well as grammar and pronunciation).
  • You receive premium learning materials for self-study between lessons: your teacher has spent hundreds of hours making high-quality digital flashcard decks for his students that target reading vocabulary, speaking vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation. As a 10-lesson package student you recieve access to a premium digital flashcard product of your choosing.
  • Don't speak English? No problem. Multilingual support can be provided in: English, German, Spanish, Portuguese, French and Japanese.

Reasons for not booking lessons with The Swedish Polyglot

  • You expect traditional teaching methods: your teacher has a background in psychology (licenced psychologist) and language learning, and does not come from a traditional teaching background. Your teacher will teach you Swedish based on how languages are learned and not based on how they are traditionally taught.

Special considerations for different groups:

  • Your teacher is a psychologist working within the health care system. Therefore, lessons may be extra interesting for medical professionals looking to achieve C1-level in order to work within the Swedish health care system.

    • Book a free consultation here with a fellow healthcare professional (your language teacher)


  • Your teacher has a background in programming. Therefore lessons may be extra interesting for programming and IT professionals who are looking to take their Swedish to the next level for their career or for other reasons.
